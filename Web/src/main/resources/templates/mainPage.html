- "In Denmark alone, we each year waste 700,000 tons of food that could have been eaten. The food waste costs Danish consumers more than 11 billion DKK a year. The households waste more than 260,000 tons of food per year and the food industry 133,000 tons. That is why food waste is a huge problem, which is not only expensive, but also exhausting of our resources and our environment. More than one third of the damaging impact on the environment from food production is caused by food waste and, therefore, could have been avoided – or alternatively used for global communities, who does not have adequate amounts of nutritional food."
